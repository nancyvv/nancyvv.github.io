About Me

My name is Nancy Valencia (nancyvv on GitHub) and I am a Web Designer and Developer.

I was born and raised in a small border town in Mexico, but lived in Mexico City, Guadalajara, Paris, and now, in the United States.

My background is in Communication and Translation Studies, but most of my working experience has been in web related projects. I started creating websites for news publications, and now, I manage web projects for a University.

My interests include Web design, User Experience and Web Development. My goal is to become a Full-Stack Developer and focus on specialized, complex web projects that combine design, interactivity and functionality.
